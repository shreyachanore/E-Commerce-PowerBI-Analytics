# E-Commerce Power BI Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end **E-Commerce Sales Analysis** using **Microsoft Power BI**.  
The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders understand **business performance**, **operational efficiency**, and **customer behavior** through interactive and data-driven insights.

The analysis focuses on solving real-world business problems such as:
- Tracking overall sales performance
- Identifying operational bottlenecks
- Understanding customer purchasing behavior
- Supporting data-driven decision-making

##  Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Microsoft Excel  

---

##  Dataset Information
The dataset contains transactional e-commerce sales data, including:
- Order ID and order date  
- Sales revenue and quantity  
- Customer and product details  
- Payment methods  
- Delivery and order status  
- Customer ratings  

This dataset is used to analyze both **business performance** and **customer behavior**.

---

##  Data Preparation
The following data preparation steps were performed:
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query  
- Handling missing and inconsistent values  
- Standardizing date and numeric formats  
- Creating calculated columns and measures using DAX  
- Designing KPIs for business reporting

## Dashboard Pages & Analysis

###  Executive Overview
**Purpose:**  
Provides a high-level view of overall business performance.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Total Sales, Total Orders, Quantity Sold, Average Order Value  
- Sales trends over time  
- Revenue contribution overview  

**Business Problem Solved:**  
Helps management quickly assess **overall business health** and track growth performance.

---

###  Operations & Customer Analysis
**Purpose:**  
Evaluates operational efficiency and customer interaction with the business.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Order status trends  
- Delivery performance  
- Customer activity patterns  
- Payment method preferences  

**Business Problem Solved:**  
Identifies **operational gaps**, delivery issues, and customer experience challenges.

---

###  Product & Customer Behavior Analysis
**Purpose:**  
Focuses on product performance and customer purchasing behavior.

**Analysis Includes:**
- Product-wise sales performance  
- Customer buying patterns  
- Repeat purchase insights  
- Rating-based customer satisfaction analysis  

**Business Problem Solved:**  
Supports **product strategy**, inventory planning, and customer retention decisions.

---

##  Key Business Insights
- Identified top-performing products and customer segments  
- Observed sales and demand patterns across different periods  
- Analyzed customer payment preferences  
- Highlighted opportunities to improve delivery and customer satisfaction
